# Principles

These principles guide the evolution of the company.json specification.

## Simplicity

The specification should be easy for humans to understand and easy for software to implement.

## Stability

Breaking changes should be extremely rare.

## Openness

The specification is open and available to everyone.

## Interoperability

The specification should work across industries, organizations, and software platforms.

## Human Authority

company.json represents information published by the organization itself.

## Extensibility

New capabilities should be added without breaking existing implementations.
